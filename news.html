    Recent 2026 publications

    Several recent papers have appeared in journals at the interface of cryptography, coding theory, cloud security and information protection:

    • “Contextual Cloud Steganography (CCS): Breaking the Capacity-Security Trade-Off”, with S. W. Mossebo Tcheunteu, L. Moyou Metcheka, S. G. R. Ekodeck, R. Ndoundam and A. Tchana, published in IEEE Transactions on Cloud Computing. DOI
    • “High embedding capacity text steganography using optimal color combinations from 24-bit space”, with S. W. Mossebo Tcheunteu, S. G. R. Ekodeck, L. Moyou Metcheka, J. M. A. Moto and R. Ndoundam, published in Journal of Computer Security. DOI
    • “Generalized subspace subcodes in the rank metric”, with O. Ndiaye and P. A. Kidoudou, published in Journal of Algebra Combinatorics Discrete Structures and Applications, 13(2): 199–216. DOI
